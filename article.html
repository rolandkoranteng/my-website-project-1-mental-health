Self-Check: Assess Your Mental State

Take a moment to reflect on your mental well-being. Answering these questions honestly can help you gain insight into your current state and identify areas where you may need support or self-care.

  1. How have you been feeling emotionally over the past week?
  2. Are you experiencing any stress, anxiety, or sadness more than usual?
  3. How well are you sleeping lately?
  4. Do you feel connected to friends, family, or a support network?
  5. What activities help you relax or feel better?
